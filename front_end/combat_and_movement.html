Different Lock-on Assists

Soft lock

The reticle automatically targets enemies on screen. when there are multiple enemies, the reticle will automatically switch to the closest enemy or the enemy closest to the center of the screen.

Hard lock

When enabled, your AC will automatically lock its orientation towards enemies as you approach them. Moving the mouse will make you switch targets.

Missile Locks

Missile Lock Description

A yellow bar will gradually fill, once fully locked a number will be displayed indicating how many missiles will be launched. firing the missiles when the lock-on gauge isn't full will lead them to miss.

Multi Missile Lock

Missiles that have multi-lock, allows you to attack multiple targets. depending on the shoulder missiles will determine how many targets

Movement

Assault Boost

It allows you to rapidly move in the direction that the camera is facing which also applies to hard-lock since the camera is facing your target

Quick Boost

Accelerates in any direction helping you evade attacks or close the distance depending on the weight and booster choice will determine the speed and distance

Tank Drift

Tank AC's can drift to quickly change the direction they are facing allowing you to avoid projectiles, the drifts can only occur while on the ground.

Tetra Hover

Tetrapods can switch to Hover mode, allowing them to remain suspended in the air draining their EN overtime while in Hover Mode, Quick Boost capabilities are Drastically increased.
